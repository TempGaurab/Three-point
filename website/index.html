About Our Project

The Challenge

Healthcare systems worldwide face an unprecedented convergence of challenges: escalating cyber threats, the need for ethical AI implementation, and a critical shortage of professionals trained at the intersection of Health Informatics, Cybersecurity, and Artificial Intelligence.

Our Solution

We're developing a comprehensive interdisciplinary program that bridges these critical domains through innovative curriculum design, hands-on training, and cutting-edge research. Our pilot course integrates theoretical foundations with practical applications, preparing the next generation of healthcare technology professionals.

Why It Matters

As healthcare becomes increasingly digital, the intersection of HI, CS, and AI becomes mission-critical. Our interdisciplinary approach ensures that future professionals can navigate complex challenges, implement secure systems, and harness AI's potential while maintaining ethical standards and patient safety.

Curriculum & Courses

Interdisciplinary Course Design

Our proposed curriculum integrates three critical domains to prepare the next generation of healthcare technology professionals:

Health Informatics Foundation

Electronic health records, healthcare data standards, clinical decision support systems, and healthcare workflow optimization.

Cybersecurity Essentials

Healthcare-specific security frameworks, risk assessment, incident response, and regulatory compliance (HIPAA, HITECH).

AI in Healthcare

Machine learning applications, ethical AI deployment, bias detection, and regulatory considerations for healthcare AI.

Integration & Practice

Hands-on projects combining all three domains, case studies, and real-world problem-solving scenarios.
