Bridging Health Informatics, Cybersecurity, and AI

Integrating disciplines to secure healthcare's digital future

A pioneering project addressing the critical need for interdisciplinary education that combines Health Informatics, Cybersecurity, and Artificial Intelligence to protect healthcare systems and enable ethical AI deployment.

Learn More Get Involved

Project Overview

The Problem

  • Shortage of integrated HI-CS-AI educational programs
  • Increasing cyber vulnerabilities in healthcare IT systems
  • Lack of hands-on training for cross-disciplinary skills
  • Growing need for ethical and secure AI implementation

Our Solution

  • Development of pilot interdisciplinary course
  • Comprehensive curriculum design
  • Textbook outline for academic resources
  • Stakeholder engagement and feedback integration

Significance

  • Addresses critical workforce development needs
  • Enhances healthcare system security
  • Promotes ethical AI deployment
  • Bridges academic and industry requirements

Curriculum & Courses

Interdisciplinary Course Design

Our proposed curriculum integrates three critical domains to prepare the next generation of healthcare technology professionals:

Health Informatics Foundation

Electronic health records, healthcare data standards, clinical decision support systems, and healthcare workflow optimization.

Cybersecurity Essentials

Healthcare-specific security frameworks, risk assessment, incident response, and regulatory compliance (HIPAA, HITECH).

AI in Healthcare

Machine learning applications, ethical AI deployment, bias detection, and regulatory considerations for healthcare AI.

Integration & Practice

Hands-on projects combining all three domains, case studies, and real-world problem-solving scenarios.
